⚔️ The Legionary ⚔️

A museum game about the Roman soldier

Who was a legionary?
A legionary was a soldier in the mighty Roman army. He wore heavy equipment to protect himself and defeat his enemies. Without the right equipment, he could not survive in battle!

🎮 How to play?

1
Look at the equipment on the right. Each item has a name and description.
2
Drag each item to the correct spot on the soldier. Correct = green, wrong = red!
3
The timer starts when you begin. The faster you finish, the higher you score!
4
Enter your name on the scoreboard and challenge your friends!
